The FBA Prep Process

Our FBA prep process is designed to be seamless and efficient, whether you are an AMAZON SELLER or an AMAZON VENDOR:

1. Send your inventory to us:

Whether you have large quantities or smaller, more frequent shipments, we accommodate all inventory sizes.

2. Prepping for Amazon:

We handle the entire preparation process, from building the shipment to confirming weights and dimensions, applying labels, and notifying you when the order is ready for dispatch.

3. Efficient Forwarding to Amazon Fulfilment Centres:

Once your shipment is prepared, we can arrange box or pallet forwarding for FBA, including UPS collection for Small Parcel Delivery, and handle LTL, FTL, and SPD deliveries using Amazon's or our own carriers.
